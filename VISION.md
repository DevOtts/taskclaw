# TaskClaw Vision

## Mission

TaskClaw is an open-source, AI-powered task management platform that helps
individuals and teams organize their work, leverage AI assistance, and
integrate with the tools they already use — all while keeping data under
their control.

## Core Principles

### Privacy-First
Your data stays on your infrastructure. Self-host TaskClaw and maintain full
control over your tasks, conversations, and integrations.

### Integration-Friendly
Work where your work already lives. TaskClaw connects to Notion, ClickUp,
and more through a clean adapter system that the community can extend.

### Community-Driven
The best integrations and features come from the people who use them. Our
adapter architecture is designed to make contributing new integrations
straightforward.

### AI-Enhanced, Not AI-Dependent
AI features help you work smarter — chat with your tasks, get suggestions,
auto-categorize. But TaskClaw works perfectly without AI too.

## Roadmap

### Near Term
- More integrations: Jira, Trello, Asana, Linear (community contributions welcome)
- Improved onboarding experience
- Better documentation and tutorials

### Medium Term
- Mobile app (React Native)
- Local LLM support (Ollama, llama.cpp)
- Automation workflows (if-this-then-that for tasks)
- Webhooks and API for external automation

### Long Term
- Plugin system for custom extensions
- Self-hosted AI embeddings for knowledge search
- Calendar integration (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- Time tracking
